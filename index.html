A bit more about me

Striving to produce high-quality, responsive apps and websites that engage users from all walks of life.

I first became interested in developing engaging and effective sites while serving as a recruiter in various industries and markets. Through first-hand experience, I understood how critical it is for web sites and mobile apps to be intuitive, responsive, pleasing to the eye, and most of all, effective.

In 2016, I attended a workshop on SEO optimization, delivered by a leading web design firm in Manhattan and I became hooked. I proceeded to self-teach basic HTML and CSS as a side-hobby which led to further exploration into the field of UX & UI design. By early 2017, I began to source tech talent and soon discovered I was ready to dive into the deep end of web development.

On October 2017, I completed the General Assembly Web Development Immersive course. WDI is an intense, 13-week, +600-hour launchpad for career-changers trained as full-stack web developers. My experience in the course led to a dynamic study and practice of foundational web development skills, applying the learnings acquired in HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Ruby, APIs, React, and much more to projects to be showcased here.

I continue to sharpen my tools in all areas of web dev and look forward to bringing my talents to your next project. In my spare time, I also enjoy sharing my expertise in talent acquistion and career coaching to hiring managers and job-seekers alike.
